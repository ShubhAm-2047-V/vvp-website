About V.V.P.

Pioneering Technical Excellence

Established with a vision to nurture competent engineering professionals, V.V.P. Polytechnic has been a cornerstone of quality education in Solapur since 2007.

Academic Achievements

Our institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and recognized by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra. Affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Mumbai, we maintain a history of stellar academic outcomes and board ranks.

Check if you meet the requirements for admission. We offer direct entries for qualified candidates.

Enrollment Overview

Admission is open to all students conforming to DTE Maharashtra regulations. The process is completely transparent and merit-based.

First-Year Admission Passed SSC (Class 10) from MSBSHSE or equivalent with minimum 35% aggregate marks. Must have studied Science, Math, and English.
Direct Second-Year Admission Passed HSC (Class 12) Science, 12th Vocational/Technical, or completed 2-year ITI course in appropriate trades.
Document Prerequisites SSC Marksheet, Leaving Certificate, Nationality/Domicile Certificate, Category Certificate (if applicable), Income Certificate, and passport photographs.
